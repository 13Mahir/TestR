Platform Features

Everything your institution needs

From exam creation to result publishing, EMS handles the complete lifecycle of academic assessments.

Exam Creation

Build exams with mixed MCQ and subjective questions. Configure timers, negative marking, and passing marks per exam.

Auto Grading

MCQ answers are graded instantly on submission. Subjective answers go to a teacher review queue. Negative marking supported.

Live Proctoring

Fullscreen enforcement, tab-switch detection, and periodic camera snapshots. Violation logs shown to teachers before grading.

Results & Analytics

Students see subject-wise and time-wise performance charts. Teachers get grade books with CSV and PDF export. Full transcripts available.

Security First

JWT auth with HttpOnly cookies, concurrent session prevention, IP logging, force password reset, and a complete admin audit trail.

Discussion Forum

A global discussion forum accessible to all roles. Admins and teachers can pin, lock, and moderate threads.
